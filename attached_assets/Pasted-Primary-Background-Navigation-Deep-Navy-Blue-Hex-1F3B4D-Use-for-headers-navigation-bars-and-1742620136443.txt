Primary Background & Navigation:
Deep Navy Blue

Hex: #1F3B4D

Use for headers, navigation bars, and major background sections to instill trust and a professional tone.

Action & Interactive Elements:
Cricket Green

Hex: #2E8B57

Ideal for buttons, icons, and call-to-action elements that need to pop.

Accents & Highlights:
Golden Yellow

Hex: #FFC107

Perfect for notifications, highlights, and hover states to inject energy and warmth.

Content Background:
Crisp White

Hex: #FFFFFF

Use as the main canvas for content and posts for maximum readability.

Secondary/Surface Background:
Light Gray

Hex: #F5F5F5

For card backgrounds and secondary sections that need a subtle separation from the main content.

Text Color:
Charcoal Gray

Hex: #333333

Provides high readability for body text without the harshness of black.

Typography
Headings & Titles:
Montserrat

Weights: Semi-Bold (for primary headings) and Bold (for emphasis)

Sizes: 24-32px depending on the hierarchy

Body & Details:
Open Sans (or Roboto as an alternative)

Weights: Regular for standard text, Medium for subheadings

Sizes: 16-18px with ample line spacing for easy reading

UI Elements & Layout
Navigation Bar:
Fixed at the top with Deep Navy Blue. Icons and active states should use Cricket Green and Golden Yellow for clear visual feedback.

Cards & Content Blocks:
Use a mix of Crisp White for primary content areas and Light Gray for card surfaces. Rounded corners and subtle shadows add a modern feel.

Buttons & Interactive Components:
Rounded buttons in Cricket Green or Golden Yellow, with smooth micro-interactions (like ripple effects) to confirm actions.

Imagery & Icons:
Integrate custom, minimalistic cricket-related icons (bats, balls, wickets) and use subtle gradients for a modern twist.

Overall Look and Feel
Modern & Clean:
The design is sleek, uncluttered, and visually balanced, ensuring that content (from networking posts to live match updates) is the focal point.

Dynamic & Engaging:
With strategic use of accent colors and interactive feedback, the app feels lively and responsive.

Accessible & Intuitive:
High contrast between text and backgrounds, responsive element sizing, and clear navigation make the app usable across all devices.
